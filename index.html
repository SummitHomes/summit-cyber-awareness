🔑 Quick Safety Tips

  • 📧 Avoid Phishing: Check sender & hover over links. Verify urgent requests via trusted channels before sharing info.
  • 👥 Prevent Impersonation: Confirm odd requests by calling directly. Watch for urgent or suspicious vibes.
  • 🤖 Mitigate AI Risks: Stick to approved tools like Copilot. No sensitive data in external AI. Report issues to IT.
  • 💬 Secure Tools: Verify group members & scan links/attachments. Use only approved platforms like Teams.
  • 🔄 Update Regularly: Enable auto-updates for software & apps to patch vulnerabilities fast.
